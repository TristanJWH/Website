About

In this section I will talk about me and this website, what it is, what it isn't and some more information about me.

What this Website is

What this website is:

  • A place for me to store information
  • A place for me to practice my HTML skills (which are admittedly limited)
  • And finally, a place to share information

What this website is not:

  • A place providing previously unknown information
  • Necessarily correct, I won't always have all of the facts
  • A website that can be relied on to exist into the future

What this Website is About

This website is perhaps not as well defined in its goals as many other websites The short answer is that it will be about whatever I want it to be about. This will mean that it will largely cover my interest but I will be keeping it roughly sectioned out mostly to make it easier to find information that you care out.

I will cover (roughly):

  • Computer programs (mostly open-source)
  • Linux
  • Programing (but don't expect anything particularly advanced)
  • Other computer-related projects

The Layout

The subjects that I cover will be sectioned out into a blog and an informational area. These sections will be linked together by the navigation bar. The informational area will be further broken down by subject and then the topic that is covered. The blog section will be broken down with articles that will cover specific areas.

Things to Expect

My website will have a few differences between it and other websites and it will be up to you to decide whether you think they are good or bad and so whether to stay around.

Technical Details

This section is probably of no interest to most people so I will keep it short it is about the technical details about this website. I am hosting this website on Vultr and have my domain name with Epik. I am writing the website with HTML and CSS but no javascript. This means that there are no trackers on this site (but this is not a promise) I did this because I like my privacy so I didn't want to take yours. Also, I am using Bootstrap in this website because it makes producing a good enough looking website very quick and easy even if it is sort of cheating.
